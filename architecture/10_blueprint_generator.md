# 10 — Blueprint Generator

> Phase 4: The actionable deliverable. Transforms all validated data into a complete, ready-to-execute business blueprint. Includes product design, revenue model, distribution plan, legal framework, and Day 1 launch using warm leads.

---

## Blueprint Sections

Each blueprint for a validated opportunity contains these sections:

```
BLUEPRINT: [Opportunity Name]
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Sections:
  1. Executive Summary
  2. Market Analysis
  3. Product Design & Format Multiplication
  4. Revenue Stress Test
  5. Monetization Architecture
  6. Distribution Strategy
  7. Day 1 Launch Plan (Using Warm Leads)
  8. Legal & Risk Assessment
  9. Product Architecture as Legal Shield
  10. Build Plan & Timeline
```

## Phase 5: Final Recommendation

After blueprints are generated for 2-3 opportunities, the system selects ONE to execute first:

### Selection Criteria

| Factor | Weight | Calculation |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Validated score | 30% | Highest score wins |
| Revenue velocity | 25% | Fastest to first dollar wins |
| Warm lead count + temperature | 20% | Most hot leads wins |
| Passive income ratio | 15% | Highest passivity wins |
| Legal simplicity | 10% | Highest legal risk score wins (less complex = better) |

### Selection Output

```
FINAL RECOMMENDATION
════════════════════

Rank | Opportunity          | Score | Velocity | Hot Leads | Passive | Legal
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
#1   | [Name]               | [X]   | [X/5]    | [N]       | [%]    | [X/5]
#2   | [Name]               | [X]   | [X/5]    | [N]       | [%]    | [X/5]
#3   | [Name]               | [X]   | [X/5]    | [N]       | [%]    | [X/5]

RECOMMENDED FIRST BUILD: #[N] — [Name]
REASON: [1-2 sentences explaining why this is the best first product]

PORTFOLIO SEQUENCE:
  Build #1 first → launch → start earning
  Build #2 next  → cross-sell to #1 audience
  Build #3 later → expand portfolio
```

## Portfolio View

When multiple opportunities pass validation, assess them as a portfolio:

```
PORTFOLIO ANALYSIS
══════════════════

AUDIENCE OVERLAP:
  [Product A] ↔ [Product B]: [High/Medium/Low] overlap ([description])
  [Product A] ↔ [Product C]: [High/Medium/Low] overlap
  [Product B] ↔ [Product C]: [High/Medium/Low] overlap

CONTENT LEVERAGE:
  Research from [Product A] reusable for [Product B]: [%]

CROSS-SELL POTENTIAL:
  [Product A] buyer → [Product B]: [estimated %] conversion
  
BRAND COHERENCE:
  All products serve: [common audience description]
  Brand positioning: "[one sentence — what you're known for]"

SEQUENCING RECOMMENDATION:
  1. [Product] — fastest to launch, proves the market
  2. [Product] — natural next step, cross-sell opportunity
  3. [Product] — portfolio expansion, recurring revenue
```
