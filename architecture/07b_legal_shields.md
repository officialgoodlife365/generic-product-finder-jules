# 07b — Legal Shields & Protection Framework

> Product architecture designed as a legal shield. Covers gated delivery, Terms of Service design, positioning language rules by niche, the 10-point legal protection checklist, auto-disqualify industries, international considerations, and the final legal verdict.

## 6. Product Architecture as Legal Shield

Design the product delivery to **maximize legal protection by design**:

### 6.1 Gated Delivery

```
Purchase → Landing page explains product scope (what it IS and ISN'T)
  → User clicks "I understand this is NOT professional advice" [checkbox]
  → User accepts Terms of Service [checkbox required]
  → Access granted to product portal / download
```

Every step creates a documented record that the buyer acknowledged the limitations.

### 6.2 Click-to-Accept Terms of Service

ToS must include:
- Product scope (educational/tool — NOT professional advice)
- Disclaimer of liability for outcomes
- User acknowledgment they should consult a professional for specific situations
- Limitation of liability (max refund of purchase price)
- Governing law and jurisdiction
- Indemnification clause

### 6.3 Language Rules

| ✅ SAY | 🔴 NEVER SAY |
|--------|-------------|
| "This guide explains..." | "You should..." (unless clearly subjective) |
| "This template is designed to help you organize..." | "Use this to file your..." |
| "Common approaches include..." | "The correct approach is..." |
| "Many small businesses find that..." | "You must do X to be compliant" |
| "Check with your [CPA/attorney/advisor]" | "This is all you need" |
| "This is for educational purposes" | "This replaces professional advice" |
| "Based on publicly available information as of [date]" | "This is guaranteed to be current/accurate" |

### 6.4 Version Dating

- Every piece of content must include: "Based on information available as of [DATE]"
- Annual (or more frequent) reviews with update dates
- Old versions archived, not deleted (shows diligence)

### 6.5 Limitation Scope Statements

For each product section, include brief scope notes:

```
📋 SCOPE: This section covers [Topic X] as described in [Regulation].
This is a summary for educational purposes. For your specific situation,
consult a qualified [CPA / attorney / compliance specialist].
Last updated: [Date]
```

## 7. Positioning Language Guide

### Per-Niche Language Rules

#### Tax & Accounting
| ✅ Safe | 🔴 Avoid |
|--------|---------|
| "Tax education guide" | "Tax advice" |
| "Organization templates for tax records" | "Tax preparation kit" |
| "Understanding common deductions" | "Deductions you should take" |
| "How to prepare for your CPA meeting" | "How to file your taxes yourself" |
| "Estimated tax calculator" (with "estimate only" labels) | "Your exact tax liability" |

#### Real Estate / Compliance
| ✅ Safe | 🔴 Avoid |
|--------|---------|
| "Compliance awareness guide" | "Compliance solution" |
| "Reporting deadline tracker" | "Filing system" |
| "Understanding FinCEN requirements" | "Complete your FinCEN filing" |
| "Checklist of what professionals typically review" | "Everything you need to comply" |

#### Health / Supplements
| ✅ Safe | 🔴 Avoid |
|--------|---------|
| "Wellness education" | "Health advice" |
| "Understanding supplement labels" | "What supplements you should take" |
| "FDA labeling requirements guide" | "Compliant label generator" |

#### Financial Services
| ✅ Safe | 🔴 Avoid |
|--------|---------|
| "Financial literacy education" | "Financial advice" |
| "Budgeting templates" | "Investment strategy template" |

## 8. Legal Protection Framework (10-Point Checklist)

Every product launched through this system must pass this checklist:

| # | Protection | Implementation | Priority |
|---|-----------|---------------|----------|
| 1 | **Terms of Service** | Click-to-accept before product access. Covers: scope, disclaimer, limitation of liability, indemnification. | 🔴 Must have |
| 2 | **Privacy Policy** | Clear data collection and usage disclosure. GDPR-compliant if serving EU. | 🔴 Must have |
| 3 | **Product Scope Statement** | Prominent on sales page AND inside product: what this IS and ISN'T. | 🔴 Must have |
| 4 | **Professional Consultation Disclaimer** | "Consult a qualified [professional] for your specific situation" — on every section. | 🔴 Must have |
| 5 | **Date Stamp** | "Information current as of [DATE]" on all content. | 🟡 Should have |
| 6 | **Version Control** | Dated versions, changelog, old versions archived. | 🟡 Should have |
| 7 | **E&O Insurance** | Professional liability insurance if available for product type. | 🟡 Should have (if available and affordable) |
| 8 | **Refund Policy** | Clear, accessible, 30-day standard. Reduces FTC risk and chargebacks. | 🔴 Must have |
| 9 | **Attorney Review** | One-time review by attorney specializing in the niche before launch. Estimated cost: $500-2,000. | Recommended for Tier B/C products |
| 10 | **Testimonial Compliance** | Real results only, typical results disclaimer, FTC endorsement guidelines followed. | 🔴 Must have (if using testimonials) |

### How to Brief an Attorney

If attorney review is recommended:

```
ATTORNEY BRIEF
──────────────
Product: [name and description]
Format: [PDF / course / templates / SaaS]
Niche: [industry vertical]
Target customer: [who is buying this]
What it does: [specific functions]
What it DOESN'T do: [clear limitations]

Questions for attorney:
1. Does this product require any professional license to sell?
2. Are our disclaimers sufficient for the product scope?
3. Are there specific regulations we should be aware of in this niche?
4. Does our Terms of Service adequately limit liability?
5. Do you recommend E&O insurance for this product type?
6. Are there any state-specific considerations for multi-state sales?

Estimated attorney cost: $500-2,000 (one-time review)
When to engage: Before launch (2-4 weeks lead time)
Where to find: State bar referral, Avvo, niche-specific legal directories
```

## Auto-Disqualify Industries

These product categories are auto-killed regardless of revenue potential:

| Industry | Why | Specific Risk |
|----------|-----|---------------|
| **Direct healthcare advice** | Practicing medicine without a license | State medical practice acts, FDA, personal injury liability |
| **Personal financial advice** | Securities regulation, fiduciary liability | SEC registration requirements, state investment advisor laws |
| **Direct legal advice** | Unauthorized practice of law | State bar association enforcement, malpractice exposure |
| **Child-facing products** | COPPA compliance, heightened liability | FTC COPPA enforcement, special privacy requirements |
| **Insurance/lending products** | Heavy state-by-state licensing | Insurance commissioner, banking regulator enforcement |
| **Pharmaceutical dosing** | Direct patient safety risk | FDA enforcement, personal injury and wrongful death liability |

**Note:** Products ABOUT these industries (education, organization, templates) may still be viable at Tier A-B. The kill applies to products that PROVIDE the professional service itself.

## International Legal Considerations

| Jurisdiction | Key Considerations |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **EU (GDPR)** | Right to erasure, explicit consent for data processing, data processing agreement needed, DPO requirements if processing at scale |
| **UK** | UK GDPR (post-Brexit equivalent), Consumer Rights Act 2015 (digital content provisions), ASA advertising standards |
| **India** | Information Technology Act, Consumer Protection Act 2019, new Personal Data Protection Bill provisions |
| **Australia** | Australian Consumer Law, Privacy Act 1988, ACCC enforcement powers |
| **Canada** | PIPEDA/provincial privacy laws, Competition Act, CASL (anti-spam) |

## Legal Risk Verdict Summary

| Verdict | Criteria | Next Step |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| ✅ **CLEAR** | Legal Risk score ≥ 4, Disclaimer Tier A, Precedent scan clean, Insurance available | Proceed to smoke test |
| ⚠️ **GUARDED** | Legal Risk score = 3, Disclaimer Tier B, No dangerous precedents, Insurance available | Proceed with attorney review recommended |
| 🟤 **HIGH RISK** | Legal Risk score = 2, Disclaimer Tier C, Precedents exist but mitigatable | Conditional — major product architecture changes required + attorney review mandatory |
| 🔴 **KILL** | Legal Risk score ≤ 1, OR Disclaimer Tier D, OR unmitigatable precedents, OR uninsurable | Do not proceed — no mitigation possible |
