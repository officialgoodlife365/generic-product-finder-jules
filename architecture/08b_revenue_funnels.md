## 5. Hybrid Funnel Model

### Full Funnel Architecture

```
LEAD MAGNET (Free)
  │  Free checklist, cheat sheet, calculator
  │  Purpose: Capture email, demonstrate value
  │  Conversion: 20-40% of visitors
  ▼
STARTER PRODUCT ($27-47)
  │  Template pack, quick guide
  │  Purpose: First purchase, low commitment, prove quality
  │  Conversion: 3-8% of email list
  ▼
ORDER BUMP ($17-37)
  │  Add-on at checkout (e.g., additional templates, cheat sheet)
  │  Purpose: Increase AOV
  │  Conversion: 20-40% of buyers
  ▼
CORE PRODUCT ($97-197)
  │  Comprehensive guide, course, or bundle
  │  Purpose: Primary revenue product
  │  Conversion: 5-15% of starter buyers
  ▼
UPSELL ($197-497)
  │  Premium version, advanced content, state-specific guides
  │  Purpose: High-ticket revenue
  │  Conversion: 10-20% of core buyers
  ▼
SUBSCRIPTION ($19-99/month)
  │  Annual updates, community access, ongoing tools
  │  Purpose: Recurring revenue
  │  Conversion: 5-15% of buyers
```

### Revenue Per Funnel (Worked Example)

```
Assume: 1,000 visitors/month

Lead magnet signups:     300 (30% opt-in)
Starter purchases:       15-24 (5-8% of list)
Order bumps:             5-10 (33% of buyers) × $27 = $135-270
Core product purchases:  2-4 (10-15% of starter) × $147 = $294-588
Upsells:                 1-2 (15% of core) × $297 = $297-594
Subscriptions:           1-2 (10% of buyers) × $29/mo = $29-58/mo

First month revenue (conservative): $500-1,200
Monthly revenue after 6 months (with list growth): $3,000-8,000
```

### Order Bump Strategy (Enhanced — from Affiliate Learnings)

The order bump appears on the checkout page as a checkbox add-on:

```
ORDER BUMP TEMPLATE
═══════════════════

□ YES! Add the [Quick-Start Template Pack] for just $17 (normally $47)

  ✓ 12 plug-and-play templates
  ✓ Fill-in-the-blank worksheets
  ✓ Saves 5+ hours of setup time

This is a ONE-TIME offer at this price. Not available after checkout.
```

**Target conversion rate:** 30-45% of buyers add the order bump.

### One-Time Offer (OTO) Strategy (Enhanced — from Affiliate Learnings)

```
OTO SEQUENCE
═══════════════

Post-checkout → Thank-you page with countdown timer

OTO 1 (30-second countdown):
  → Premium version OR advanced content
  → Price: 1.5-3× front-end price
  → If ACCEPTED → add to order, show OTO 2
  → If DECLINED → show downsell (same product, lower price OR fewer features)

OTO 2 (optional):
  → Complementary product OR done-for-you service
  → Price: $47-$197
  → If ACCEPTED → add to order
  → If DECLINED → proceed to membership portal

RULE: Maximum 2 upsells. More than 2 creates buyer fatigue.
```

## 7. Affiliate Economics

### Commission Structure

| Product Type | Standard Commission | Why |
|-------------|-------------------|-----|
| Front-end product (< $100) | 65-75% | High commission drives affiliate motivation |
| Mid-tier product ($100-300) | 50-65% | Balance between affiliate incentive and margin |
| High-ticket ($300+) | 40-50% | Lower percentage but higher dollar amount |
| Recurring subscription | 30-50% (first 3-6 months only, or lifetime) | Lifetime commissions attract top affiliates |

### Affiliate Recruitment Strategy

| Target | Where to Find | Pitch |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Niche bloggers** | Search "[niche] blog", check for affiliate programs | "We have a product your audience needs + [X]% commissions" |
| **YouTube creators** | Search "[niche] tutorial" on YouTube | Offer free product + review request + affiliate link |
| **Niche newsletter curators** | Search "[niche] newsletter" | Sponsored mention + affiliate link |
| **Community influencers** | From warm lead pipeline (influence_tier = "influencer") | Already identified during discovery — natural fit |

### Affiliate Reverse-Engineering

Search existing affiliate products in the niche to understand what converts:

```
ClickBank: Browse [niche] category → sort by Gravity (sales indicator)
  → What products sell? What sales pages work? What price points convert?

JVZoo: Browse marketplace → filter by [niche] → sort by sales
  → What funnels exist? What upsell paths work?

Top affiliate blogs: Find affiliates promoting competitor products
  → What copy do they use? What audiences respond?
  → Could these same affiliates promote your product?
```

## 8. LTV Modeling

### LTV Formula

```
LTV = Front-End Revenue
    + (Upsell Revenue × Upsell Rate)
    + (Subscription Price × Avg Retention Months × Subscription Rate)
    + (Cross-Sell Revenue × Cross-Sell Rate)

Example:
  Front-end: $97
  Upsell: $297 × 15% = $44.55
  Subscription: $29/mo × 8 months × 10% = $23.20
  Cross-sell: $127 × 5% = $6.35

  LTV = $97 + $44.55 + $23.20 + $6.35 = $171.10
```

### LTV:CAC Analysis

| Ratio | Verdict | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **> 5:1** | Excellent | Scale aggressively — increase ad spend, recruit more affiliates |
| **3:1 to 5:1** | Healthy | Standard scaling — sustainable growth |
| **2:1 to 3:1** | Tight | Optimize conversion OR reduce CAC before scaling |
| **< 2:1** | Unprofitable | Must improve product (increase LTV) OR find cheaper channels (reduce CAC) |

## 9. Recurring Revenue Strategies

### Subscription Models by Product Type

| Product | Subscription Model | Pricing | Retention Driver |
|---------|-------------------|---------|-----------------| 
| **Compliance guide** | Annual update subscription | $97/year or $12/month | Regulations change annually — customers MUST update |
| **Template pack** | Template expansion + updates | $9-29/month | New templates monthly, keep existing ones current |
| **Calculator/tool** | SaaS access fee | $9-49/month | Access to tool is ongoing need |
| **Community** | Membership | $19-99/month | Peer support, expert Q&A, networking |
| **Course** | Course + updates access | $19/month | New modules, updated content, community access |

### Retention Tactics

| Tactic | Implementation | Expected Impact |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| **Annual prepay discount** | 20-30% discount for annual payment vs. monthly | Increases retention, reduces churn |
| **Content drip** | Release new content monthly to justify subscription | Gives reason to stay subscribed |
| **Community engagement** | Regular Q&A sessions, member showcases | Social lock-in — hard to leave community |
| **Usage reminders** | Email reminders about unused features, upcoming deadlines | Remind them of value they're paying for |
| **Exit survey + save offer** | When canceling: ask why + offer discount or pause | Saves 10-20% of cancellations |

### Dunning & Win-Back Sequence (Enhanced — from Affiliate Learnings)

```
SUBSCRIPTION RECOVERY SEQUENCE

  Day 7:  "Here's what you missed this week" → 5% return
```

## 11. Cross-Sell & Portfolio Revenue

When multiple products serve overlapping audiences:

### Cross-Sell Matrix

```
Product A customers who also buy:
  → Product B: [%] (same niche, different problem)
  → Product C: [%] (same audience, related tool)

Portfolio Revenue Multiplier: 1.0 + (cross-sell rate × number of products)
  e.g., 1.0 + (0.15 × 3 additional products) = 1.45× single-product revenue
```

### Portfolio Strategy

| Strategy | When | Expected Lift |
|----------|------|--------------|
| **Cross-sell at purchase** | "Customers also bought..." | 5-15% of buyers add a product |
| **Cross-sell via email** | After purchase, in welcome sequence | 3-8% conversion on cross-sell emails |
| **Bundle pricing** | Offer all products at discounted bundle price | Higher AOV + faster product adoption |
| **Sequence by pain** | Product A solves Problem 1 → leads to Problem 2 → Product B | Natural upgrade path |
