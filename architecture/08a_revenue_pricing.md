## 1. Revenue Velocity Optimization

Speed to first dollar is a core competitive advantage for solo founders.

### Velocity Accelerators

| Strategy | Time Savings | How |
|----------|-------------|-----|
| **Pre-built delivery platform** | Weeks → days | Use Gumroad, Systeme.io, Teachable — zero development time |
| **Template-first format** | Weeks → days | Templates are fastest to create and deliver |
| **AI-assisted creation** | 60-80% time savings | Use AI for first drafts, research synthesis, formatting |
| **Existing audience leverage** | Skip distribution ramp-up | Launch to warm leads immediately (Doc 04) |
| **Pre-sale before build** | Revenue before product exists | Validate and collect revenue simultaneously |
| **Regulatory urgency** | Natural demand accelerator | Upcoming deadline creates built-in urgency — no marketing needed |

| SaaS calculator/tool | 4-8 weeks | 8-10 weeks | Week 8-12 |

## 2. Price Anchoring Methodology

### Step 1: Find the Professional Price Anchor

Search for what professionals charge for solving this same problem:

| Source | What to Search | Example |
|--------|---------------|---------|
| **Upwork/Fiverr** | Gig pricing for this specific task | "$500-2,000 for AML compliance setup" |
| **Accounting/law firm websites** | Published fee schedules | "FinCEN compliance: $2,500-5,000" |
| **Professional associations** | Survey data on typical fees | "Average CPA fee for BOI reporting: $3,000" |
| **Reddit/forums** | People quoting prices they were charged | "My CPA wants $3,000 for this" |
| **Google** | "[service] cost" or "[service] average fees" | Industry averages |

### Step 2: Position at 10-20% of Anchor

```
PRICE ANCHORING CALCULATION
───────────────────────────
Professional service price:    $[X] (average from research)
10% of professional price:     $[Y] (floor)
20% of professional price:     $[Z] (ceiling)

Your price range:              $[Y] to $[Z]

Selected price:                $[final]
Justification:                 [why this specific price within the range]
```

### Worked Example

```
Problem: FinCEN AML compliance for small landlords
Professional price anchor: CPA charges $2,000-3,000

10% = $200-300
20% = $400-600

Competing product: $297 (overpriced, outdated)

Selected price: $197 (front-end)
Justification: Under competitor pricing, well within 10-20% range,
               psychologically below $200 threshold, room for upsell
```

### Price Psychology Rules

| Rule | Application |
|------|------------|
| **Just-under pricing** | $197 > $200, $97 > $100, $47 > $50 |
| **Anchor in marketing copy** | "CPAs charge $3,000 for this. Get it for $197." |
| **Three-tier pricing** | Starter ($47) / Standard ($97-147) / Premium ($197-297) |
| **Urgency pricing** | Launch price expires, early-bird discount, deadline-tied discount |

## 3. Format Multiplication

### Product Format Catalog

| Format | Creation Time | Price Range | Passive Ratio | Best For |
|--------|-------------|-------------|---------------|----------|
| **PDF Guide** | 2-3 weeks | $27-97 | 95% | Education, evergreen reference |
| **Template Pack** | 1-2 weeks | $27-97 | 98% | Operational workflows, compliance checklists |
| **Video Course** | 3-5 weeks | $97-497 | 90% | Complex processes, visual learners |
| **Online Calculator/Tool** | 2-8 weeks | $9-49/month | 85% | Recurring calculations, estimations |
| **Membership/Community** | 2-4 weeks setup | $19-99/month | 40-60% | Ongoing support, updates, networking |
| **Done-For-You Templates** | 1-3 weeks | $197-997 | 95% | Industry-specific documents, forms |
| **Audit/Assessment Tool** | 2-4 weeks | $47-197 | 90% | Self-assessment, compliance readiness |
| **Cheat Sheet/Quick Ref** | 2-3 days | Free (lead magnet) or $7-27 | 99% | Lead capture, low-risk entry point |
| **Email Course (Drip)** | 1-2 weeks | Free (lead magnet) or $27-47 | 95% | Lead nurturing, building authority |
| **Consultation/Coaching** | None (live) | $97-297/hour | 0% | High-touch — not recommended for passive income |

### Multiplication Sequencing Strategy

Build products in this order to maximize speed and revenue:

```
STAGE 1 (Week 1-2): Quick Win
→ Checklist or cheat sheet (free lead magnet)
→ Template pack ($47-97)
Revenue: Starts immediately

STAGE 2 (Week 3-4): Core Product
→ PDF guide ($97-197)
→ Combine with templates into a bundle
Revenue: Accelerating

STAGE 3 (Week 5-8): Scale
→ Video course ($197-497)
→ Calculator/tool (freemium or $9-29/month)
Revenue: Diversified

STAGE 4 (Month 3+): Recurring
→ Subscription for annual updates ($9-29/month)
→ Community membership ($19-49/month)
Revenue: Recurring + passive
```

### Format Multiplication Example

```
One research effort: "FinCEN AML Compliance for Small Landlords"

Format 1: Compliance Checklist (free lead magnet)        — $0 (captures leads)
Format 2: Template Pack (forms, letters, trackers)       — $47
Format 3: Complete PDF Guide (50 pages)                  — $97
Format 4: Bundle (Guide + Templates)                     — $147
Format 5: Video Walkthrough Course (3 hours)             — $297
Format 6: State-Specific Add-On Packs (50 states)        — $47 each
Format 7: Annual Update Subscription                     — $19/month or $97/year

Total addressable revenue per customer: $47 to $600+
From ONE research effort.
```

## 4. Passive Income Ratio

### Calculation

```
Passive Income Ratio = Revenue NOT requiring active time / Total Revenue × 100

Example:
  Templates: $5,000/month (zero active time)        → 100% passive
  Guide sales: $3,000/month (update 4 hours/year)   → 99% passive
  Coaching: $2,000/month (20 hours active)           → 0% passive

  Total passive: ($5,000 + $3,000) / ($5,000 + $3,000 + $2,000) = 80%
```

### Ratio Optimization Strategies

| Current Ratio | Strategy to Increase |
|---------------|---------------------|
| < 25% (service-heavy) | Productize: turn live sessions into recorded courses, create templates from consulting deliverables |
| 25-50% | Automate: self-service portal, FAQ section, automated delivery |
| 50-75% | Subscriptionize: convert one-time updates into subscription model |
| 75-90% | Systematize: automated onboarding, self-serve refunds, zero-touch delivery |
| 90%+ | Optimize: already excellent — focus on traffic and conversion optimization |
